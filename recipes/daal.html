Daal

Description:

Daal Fry is a delicious and popular Indian dish made with lentils , onions, tomatoes, spices and herbs. Daal refers to lentils and are staple in most Indian homes. Apart from being nutritious, it is a comfort food & is super easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup pigeon pea lentil (arhar/ toor)
  • Salt , to taste
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder , divided
  • 1 tbsp clarified butter , (ghee) use oil for vegan preparation
  • 1 pinch asafoetida (hing)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 dried red chilli
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 6-8 curry leaves , fresh or dried
  • 1 green chilli , chopped (optional)
  • ½ inch ginger , chopped
  • 1 clove garlic , chopped
  • 2 onions , chopped (about ½ cup)
  • 2 tomatoes , chopped (about ¾ cup)

Steps:

  1. Wash the lentils (arhar dal) in 3-4 ranges of water. In a pot, cook the dal with 1½ cups water, some salt, and ½ tsp turmeric, until it is soft.* Keep aside.
  2. In a separate pan, heat ghee** on medium heat. Add asafoetida and cumin seeds.
  3. When the seeds start to crackle, add the dried red chili, cinnamon stick, curry leaves, ginger, garlic, and green chilies. Mix well.
  4. Next, add the onions. Mix well and sauté for about 5 minutes till the onions start to brown.
  5. Add the chopped tomatoes and mix. Add the remaining turmeric and salt. Mix well. Add a little water if the masala is sticking to the pot. Cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Add the cooked dal, mix everything together and let the curry simmer for another couple of minutes.
  7. For garnish, add fresh coriander leaves and serve with rice.
